VocaScramble: Unscramble the Word

Rules:
  1. Enter scrambled words (up to 25, one per line).
  2. Enter the correct words (matching order, one per line).
  3. Set 2–3 team names.
  4. Click a card to reveal the correct word.
  5. Teams guess the word. Correct = +10 points.
  6. Next team plays the next card.
